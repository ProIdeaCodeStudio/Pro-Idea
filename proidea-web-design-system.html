05 — Brand Rules
Do's & Don'ts
✓ DO
Use Deep Ink as the primary background on all dark layouts.
✗ DON'T
Never use Amber as a background for large areas — it's an accent only.
✓ DO
Pair Syne 800 with Cormorant Italic for contrast between strength and elegance.
✗ DON'T
Never use pure white (#FFFFFF). Always use Warm White (#F5EDD8) to keep the warmth.
✓ DO
Use thin horizontal amber lines and minimal borders as design elements — your visual signature.
✗ DON'T
Never add drop shadows, gradients, or glow effects. Keep it flat and intentional.
✓ DO
Use Ash (#1E1B12) for card and section backgrounds to layer depth without going pure black.
✗ DON'T
Never mix Burnt Ember and Ember Tint in the same element — use one amber variant per component.
